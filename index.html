Pistoboltz*

Pistoboltz, MIT’s national-level symposium, unites Tamil Nadu’s aspiring engineers, fostering talent and industry connections.

Hi, I am Clive® I’m a
designer and highly talented 3D
renderer with over a decade of experience in the field.

What is pistoboltz?

Pistoboltz is a National level technical symposium conducted by Mechanical engineers of MIT for aspiring engineers. Mechanical Engineering was started in 2015 for the first time under the Department of Production Technology. Pistoboltz provides a promising platform for students from all over Tamilnadu to express their talents. Pistoboltz plays a vital role in our ever-increasing relationships with a wide range of eminent people and renowed companies

Bringing Ideas to Life

Pistoboltz endeavors to spread knowledge, skills, heritage, legacy and infrastructure of MIT and Anna University. Through this we ensure that students from the private organizations utilize and learn from the Anna University.

Innovation powerhouse

Pistoboltz is more like a social gathering, where people share and gain knowledge. Since it is an annual event, we strive to summarize and exhibit the technological developments and innovations that particular this year
